Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generation and never fails to delight

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 2 tsp groug cinamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples,peeied and sliced

Instruction

  1. preheat oven to 375F(190F)
  2. In a bowl,mix the sugar and cinnamon
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture
